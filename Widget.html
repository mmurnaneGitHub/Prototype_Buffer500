Find historic properties within 500' by clicking a parcel.

Find out what historic properties are within 500 feet of a property.


STEPS:
  1. Click parcel on map.
  2. Information will appear in the Results section below. Blue text with underlines are links to historic nomination documents. Blue text without underlines may be clicked to show historic property location.

 

DATA LIMITATIONS: This tool is designed to identify properties that are listed on the Tacoma Register of Historic Places or are within local historic districts. To find properties on the National Register of Historic Places, or the Washington State Heritage Register, or to determine whether there is a listed archaeological site within your project area, you must contact the Washington State Department of Archaeology and Historic Preservation at https://dahp.wa.gov/.
