Find historic properties within 500' by clicking a parcel.
STEPS:
  1. Click parcel on map.
  2. Information appears in the Results below.
    Blue text with underlines are links to historic nomination documents.
    Blue text without underlines may be clicked to show historic property location.

 

DATA LIMITATIONS: This tool is designed to identify properties listed on the Tacoma Register of Historic Places or within local historic districts. To find properties on the National Register of Historic Places, or the Washington State Heritage Register, or to determine whether there is a listed archaeological site within your project area, contact the Washington State Department of Archaeology and Historic Preservation at https://dahp.wa.gov/.
